Isha Yogshala

Located in the spiritual heart of Rishikesh, Isha Yogshala is dedicated to sharing the authentic wisdom of traditional yoga. Rooted in ancient yogic teachings, we offer Yoga Teacher Training, Hatha Yoga, Ashtanga Yoga, Pranayama, Meditation, and Yoga Philosophy in a supportive and inspiring environment. Our mission is to help students cultivate physical well-being, mental clarity, and inner awareness while experiencing yoga as a holistic way of life. Whether you are a beginner or an aspiring teacher, Isha Yogshala provides a transformative journey toward balance, self-discovery, and personal growth. "Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the Self."
